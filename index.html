Safe Space

A tool to document domestic violence and remain safe

Safe Space

  • JavaScript
  • Node.JS
  • Responsive

Safe Space is working mock-up for Empath360, a non-profit domestic violence center. Safe Space allows users to store information about domestic violence experiences for future court dates. It also allows users to call 911 or a safety contact with one button if they are in danger.
